Kenny Lau

I am a Physics PhD candidate working on experimental Cosmology at the University of Minnesota. My research focuses on the measurement of degree-scale B-mode polarization in the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB), and I am working in the BICEP/Keck Collaboration and CMB-S4 experiment for this observation.
